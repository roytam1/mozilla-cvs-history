Privacy and Security Preferences

This section describes how to modify the Privacy and Security preferences. To access them, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Edit menu and choose Preferences.
  2. Click the Privacy and Security category. If no subcategories are visible, click to expand the list, then click the name for the preferences you want to view or change.

For help with a panel's settings, click the Help button at the bottom of that panel.
