Privacy & Security Preferences

This section describes how to modify the Privacy & Security preferences. To access them, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Edit menu and choose Preferences.
  2. Click the Privacy & Security category. If no subcategories are visible, double-click the category to expand the list, then click the name for the preferences you want to view or change.
