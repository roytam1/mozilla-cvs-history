FAQ on Tinderbox 

Q. What is Tinderbox.
A. Your very own automated build page.  It shows you how builds are going on various platforms. fs

Q. I just checked in some code.  How can I tell when I'm OK.
A. You name will appear in the guilty column.  When there are successful (green) builds in all the columns in a row above your name, you know you are ok.

Q. The tree is broken, how do I find out what is busted (or who busted it).
A. Clicking 'L' in the first red box (first build to break) above a green will show you a build log for the broken build.  You can also click 'C' in this box and see what code was checked in.
