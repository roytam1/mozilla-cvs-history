1.6. Translations

The Bugzilla Guide needs translators! Please volunteer your translation into the language of your choice. If you will translate this Guide, please notify the members of the mozilla-webtools mailing list at <mozilla-webtools@mozilla.org>, and arrange with The Bugzilla Team to check it into CVS.
